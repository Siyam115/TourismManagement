Management Policies

Here you can find important management policies related to tourism operations, booking procedures, and user safety guidelines.

  • Booking Confirmation: All bookings must be confirmed at least 24 hours before the scheduled tour.
  • Payment Policy: A 50% deposit is required at the time of booking, with the balance due before the tour begins.
  • Refund Policy: Refunds are available for cancellations made at least 48 hours prior to the tour, subject to a processing fee.
  • Safety Guidelines: All customers must adhere to safety protocols, including wearing appropriate gear and following tour leader instructions.
  • Group Size: Tours are limited to a maximum of 15 participants to ensure safety and a personalized experience.
  • Cancellation Policy: Tours may be canceled by the management due to weather conditions or other unforeseen circumstances, with a full refund provided.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How can I book a tour?

    Follow the booking steps on the Tours page, select a package, and proceed with payment.

  • What is the refund policy?

    Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days if cancellation is made at least 48 hours before the tour.

  • What should I bring on the tour?

    It is recommended to bring comfortable shoes, a water bottle, sunscreen, and a camera.

  • Are tours available for private groups?

    Yes, we offer private tours for groups. Please contact us to customize your tour experience.

  • Is transportation provided for the tours?

    Transportation is included for most tours. Please check the specific tour details for information on transportation.

  • Can I modify or reschedule my booking?

    Yes, you can modify or reschedule your booking up to 48 hours before the tour. Additional fees may apply.
